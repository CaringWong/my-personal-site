About CaringWong

CaringWong is an illustrator and architect from China. After living and studying in Paris for several years, where she completed her Master’s degree at l'École Nationale Supérieure d'Architecture de Paris-La Villette (ENSAPLV), she is currently based in southern China. She creates illustrations for books, music, games, and various products. She has a passion for drawing the cosmos, landscapes, skies, seas，cities and cats, as well as capturing the scenes of her daydreams.
